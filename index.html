Fetchinsta

by DPR

How To Use

  • share posts from Instagram to Fetchinsta App by clicking share

  • Click *Get Post* Button in Fetchinsta App.

  • Hola... you get Posts in Fetchinsta

    • To download

  • after posts has been shown in Screen, you can see the rounded Download icon at bottom right of post

  • Click *Download icon*

  • Now, Fetchinsta asks you to grand Storage Permission to Download Post. Click Allow Permission

  • Enjoy ....post started to download

    • To share Post/Reel to WhatsApp or other App without Download

  • share posts from Instagram to Fetchinsta App by clicking share

  • Click *Get Post* Button in Fetchinsta App.

  • after Posts has been loaded successfully, *Click share icon* at bottom which is next to search icon

  • wait few seconds for the process

  • share Options will shown to you to select to where you wants to share this Post. like WhatsApp

  • Select the option.

  • Done, you have successfully shared the post without Downloading it.
